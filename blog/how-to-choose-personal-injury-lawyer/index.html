How to Choose the Right Personal Injury Lawyer for Your Case

March 5, 2026 • By Attorney Mike Zara

The attorney you hire will directly impact the outcome of your case. Whether you need a car accident lawyer, a truck accident attorney, or representation for a wrongful death claim, here are the key factors to consider.

Experience and Focus

Look for an attorney who focuses on personal injury rather than a generalist. Ask how many cases they have handled and in what practice areas. Attorney Mike Zara has dedicated nearly 20 years exclusively to personal injury law across Colorado, Arizona, Florida, Georgia, North Carolina, and Wisconsin.

Track Record

Ask about results in cases similar to yours. Check our case results page for examples of settlements and verdicts Zara Injury Law has achieved. But be wary of attorneys who guarantee specific outcomes before reviewing the facts.

Client Communication

Will you work directly with the attorney or be passed to a paralegal? At Zara Injury Law, every client receives Mr. Zara's personal cell phone number. Injuries do not happen on a 9-to-5 schedule, and your attorney should be available when you need them.

Fee Structure

Most personal injury attorneys work on contingency -- no fee unless you win. Make sure you understand whether costs are deducted before or after the attorney's percentage. At Zara Injury Law, there is no fee unless we recover compensation.

Red Flags

Be cautious of guarantees, pressure to sign immediately, or difficulty reaching the attorney. A reputable attorney offers a free consultation and gives you time to decide. Schedule your free consultation with Zara Injury Law today.
